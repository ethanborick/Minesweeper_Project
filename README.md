### Game Window
Once the player enters their name, the game window appears:

<img width="832" height="578" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-08 at 9 44 35 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/75a49641-223a-4f90-8311-fb26ac7cd617" />

The game window supports the following functionality:
- **Reset:** clicking the button with the smiley face resets the game board to its original state.
- **Debug Mode:** clicking the button with the mine reveals the location of all mines on the board.

<img width="830" height="579" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-08 at 9 44 51 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3e4fac40-844f-42d6-9f6d-75f0e190330f" />
  
- **Pause/Play:** clicking this button toggles the state of the board (normal or paused).

  <img width="833" height="580" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-08 at 9 45 07 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/00bd10ab-14c0-43c9-b70b-7750a0776d8c" />

- **Leaderboard:** clicking this button opens the leaderboard window.

  <img width="416" height="291" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-08 at 9 46 25 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/31835de1-f547-4356-85e1-6ff28825396f" />

- **Timer:** located in the bottom right of the game window.
- **Mine Counter:** located in the bottom left of the game window, indicates the number of unflagged mines remaining.
- **Revealing Tiles:** a player may click on any tile to reveal it and any adjacent tiles that are not mines.
- **Flagging Mines:** a player may right click on any tile to flag it.
